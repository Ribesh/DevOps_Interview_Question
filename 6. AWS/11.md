## Q. What is a marketplace AMI?

### Answer:

-   AMI ==> Amazon Machine Image

-   AWS Marketplace is a place where you can find external software or operating system that i can use without needing to sign a specific contract with an organization.

-   Whenever I use the software on my EC2 Instance, I have to pay 2 kinds of fees.
    1.  One for EC2 instance to AWS
    2.  Second for software or operating system to the company who owns it

-   It is a place to look for a lot of variety of softwares
